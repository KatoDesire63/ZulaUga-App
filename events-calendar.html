Popular Ugandan Festivals

Month Date Event Location Description
January Early January Nyege Nyege Festival Jinja A four-day festival celebrating music and cultural diversity from across Africa and the world.
February Mid-February Kampala City Festival Kampala The largest street party in East Africa featuring parades, music, dance, food, and cultural showcases.
March Late March Entebbe Sailing Regatta Entebbe A popular sailing competition attracting participants and spectators to the shores of Lake Victoria.
June June (Biennial) Imbalu Circumcision Ceremony Mbale A traditional circumcision ceremony among the Bagisu people, marking the rite of passage for boys into manhood.
August Early August Ekitaguriro Cultural Festival Mbarara A celebration of the Ankole culture featuring traditional music, dance, and cattle herding events.
September Mid-September Bayimba International Festival Lunkulu Island A multidisciplinary arts festival promoting music, dance, theatre, film, and visual arts.
October October 9th Uganda Independence Day Nationwide National celebrations marking Uganda's independence with parades, speeches, and cultural performances.
November Mid-November Amakula International Film Festival Kampala A platform for filmmakers to showcase their work and engage with audiences through screenings and workshops.
December Early December Pearl of Africa Tourism Expo Kampala An annual tourism expo promoting Uganda's tourism products and services, featuring exhibitors, networking events, and cultural performances.
