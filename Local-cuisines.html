Discover Ugandan Local Cuisines

Matoke

A traditional dish made from steamed green bananas, often served with peanut sauce.

Posho[Ugali]

A staple food made from maize flour, similar to polenta or grits

Luwombo

A delicious dish made with meat, pork or groundnuts, steamed in banana leaves.

Rolex

A popular street food consisting of a chapati rolled with eggs and vegetables.

Nsenene

Seasoned and fried grasshoppers,considered a delicacy and typically eaten during the rainy season.

Kikomando

A simple yet popular dish of sliced chapati mixed with fried beans or meat.
